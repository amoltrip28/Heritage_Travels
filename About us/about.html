About Heritage Travels

Connecting drivers and travelers for a better car rental experience

Our Story

Heritage Travels was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to revolutionize the car rental industry by connecting travelers directly with local drivers. We noticed a gap in the market where traditional rental companies were charging high fees while car owners had vehicles sitting idle.

Our founder, Rajesh Sharma, experienced this firsthand when he needed to rent a car for a family trip and was shocked by the prices. At the same time, his neighbor's car was sitting unused in the driveway. This sparked the idea for a platform that would benefit both parties – travelers get better rates, and car owners earn extra income.

Starting with just a handful of cars in Mumbai, we've now expanded to multiple cities across India, connecting thousands of drivers with travelers who need reliable transportation at affordable rates.

Our Mission & Values

What drives us forward every day

Customer-Centric

We put our customers first in everything we do. From designing our platform to handling support inquiries, we're committed to providing an exceptional experience for both drivers and travelers.

Trust & Safety

We prioritize the safety of our community. All drivers undergo thorough verification, and vehicles are regularly inspected to ensure they meet our quality and safety standards.

Transparency

We believe in clear, honest communication. Our pricing is straightforward with no hidden fees, and we're always upfront about our policies and procedures.

Community

We're building more than just a rental platform – we're creating a community where drivers and travelers can connect, share experiences, and help each other explore new destinations.

Innovation

We continuously strive to improve our platform and services. By embracing new technologies and listening to feedback, we're constantly evolving to better serve our users.

Accessibility

We believe everyone should have access to affordable transportation. Our platform is designed to be inclusive and accessible to all users, regardless of their background or needs.
